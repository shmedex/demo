Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

We treat a wide range of mental health conditions including depression, anxiety disorders, bipolar disorder, ADHD, PTSD, OCD, and other mood and behavioral disorders.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office during business hours or using the online booking system on our website. New patient appointments typically require a brief phone screening first.

We accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office to verify your specific coverage. We also offer self-pay options and can provide documentation for out-of-network reimbursement.

Your first appointment will be a comprehensive evaluation where we'll discuss your concerns, medical history, and treatment goals. This helps us develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.

Treatment duration varies depending on individual needs. Some patients benefit from short-term therapy (a few months), while others may require longer-term care. We regularly evaluate progress and adjust treatment as needed.
